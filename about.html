Who We Are

India's Largest Single-Site
Phthalic Anhydride Producer

IG Petrochemicals Ltd. is not a small regional player. We are India's dominant Phthalic Anhydride manufacturer — with approximately 50% domestic market share, 270,000 MTPA of installed capacity, and five PA production lines operating at our Taloja, Maharashtra facility.

Our products are critical inputs for the plasticizer, resin, dye, pigment, pharmaceutical, and coatings industries. Every major PVC pipe manufacturer, paint company, and polyester resin producer in India either sources from us or competes with those who do.

Listed on NSE and BSE, we are a publicly traded chemical company with the scale, technology, and operational depth to serve as a reliable long-term supply partner.

Operating Philosophy

How We Operate

Proven Technology, Relentlessly Optimized

We operate on Wacker Chemie GmbH's Von-Heyden Low Energy Process — the global benchmark for PA production. But we did not stop at the license. We have continuously debottlenecked, upgraded instrumentation, and fine-tuned our five plants to run at consistently high capacity utilization.

Integrated Waste-to-Value Recovery

Maleic Anhydride is recovered from PA process wash water. Benzoic Acid is captured as a byproduct. Steam generated from our exothermic reactions feeds back into the process. This is not sustainability theater — it is process economics, and it makes us a lower-cost producer.

Customer-First, Not Volume-First

We run on annual contracts with India's major plasticizer, resin, and pigment manufacturers. Our customer base is diversified across end-use industries, reducing concentration risk. We compete on reliability and consistency — not just price.

Why Our Position Matters

Structural Advantages, Not Temporary Gains

01

Scale Creates Reliability

270,000 MTPA across five plants means no single shutdown disrupts supply. A procurement manager sourcing from IGPL is not betting on a single reactor — they are accessing a diversified production base at one site.

02

Location Is a Competitive Moat

Taloja sits in Maharashtra's chemical belt, 50 km from JNPT. Our feedstock (o-xylene) arrives by pipeline and road from nearby refineries. Finished goods reach port customers with minimal freight. This geography cannot be easily replicated by a new entrant.

03

Downstream Integration Strengthens Margins

By producing DEP in-house, we capture value beyond the commodity PA cycle. Our byproducts — MA and BA — are not waste streams but revenue streams. This integrated model smooths earnings across business cycles.

04

India's PA Demand Is Growing

India's plasticizer, construction, automotive, and packaging sectors are expanding. PA is a foundational input for all of them. As India's largest PA producer, IGPL is positioned to capture a disproportionate share of that growth without needing to build greenfield capacity elsewhere.

Technology & Quality

Engineered in Germany.
Operated in India.

Our PA plants are based on the Wacker Von-Heyden Low Energy Process — originally engineered by Lurgi GmbH, Germany. This is the same technology platform used by leading PA producers globally.

We produce Phthalic Anhydride at >=99.8% purity, in snow-white free-flowing flakes that meet international specifications. Our quality control is continuous — from feedstock analysis through in-process monitoring to final product testing.
