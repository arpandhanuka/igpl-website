Downstream Applications

From Chemical Plant to Finished Product

IGPL's four core products feed into 15+ downstream industries — from the plasticizer in your PVC pipe to the resin in your car's topcoat.

Product Portfolio

Four Products. Dozens of Applications.

Every product from Taloja enters a chain of downstream formulators and manufacturers that touch industries across the economy.

Primary Product

Phthalic Anhydride (PA)

The anchor product of IGPL's portfolio, PA is the chemical backbone of plasticizers, alkyd resins, and unsaturated polyester resins used across construction, coatings, and flexible PVC applications.

  • Plasticizers — DOP, DINP, DIDP for flexible PVC
  • Alkyd resins for decorative and industrial coatings
  • Unsaturated polyester resins (UPR) for composites
  • Phthalate-free plasticizers (DINCH, DOTP)
  • Glyptal resins for electrical insulation
Paints & Coatings Flexible PVC Construction Automotive

Coproduct

Maleic Anhydride (MA)

Recovered as a coproduct in PA synthesis, MA is a versatile intermediate widely used in composites manufacturing, food-grade acids, and agrochemical synthesis.

  • Unsaturated polyester resins (UPR) for FRP
  • Fumaric acid and malic acid production
  • Agrochemicals — herbicide and pesticide synthesis
  • Lubricant additives and corrosion inhibitors
  • Specialty polymer modification
FRP Composites Food & Pharma Agrochemicals
Product detail
Coproduct

Benzoic Acid (BA)

A high-value coproduct from PA manufacture, benzoic acid serves as a food preservative precursor, pharmaceutical intermediate, and specialty chemical building block.

  • Sodium benzoate — food and beverage preservative
  • Benzoyl chloride for pharma synthesis
  • Benzaldehyde for flavor and fragrance
  • Phenol precursor in chemical synthesis
  • Plasticizers via benzoate esters
Food & Beverage Pharmaceuticals Specialty Chemicals

Downstream

Diethyl Phthalate (DEP)

Produced downstream from PA, DEP is a low-toxicity phthalate ester used as a fragrance fixative, cellulose solvent, and specialty plasticizer across personal care and pharmaceutical sectors.

  • Fragrance fixative in perfumes and cosmetics
  • Cellulose ester solvent for film and lacquer
  • Pharmaceutical tablet coating solvent
  • Insect repellent carrier solvent
  • Specialty polymer and film plasticizer

Supply Chain Position

Where IGPL Sits in the Value Chain

IGPL converts o-xylene feedstock into phthalic anhydride and recovers maleic anhydride, benzoic acid, and DEP as coproducts.

Our products are intermediates — they leave Taloja as industrial chemicals and re-enter the economy as plasticizers, resins, preservatives, solvents, and coatings in products people use daily.

This position in the middle of the chain makes IGPL structurally important. When demand for coatings or flexible PVC rises, IGPL benefits. When a new application emerges — phthalate-free plasticizers, bio-based UPR — IGPL is positioned to serve it.
