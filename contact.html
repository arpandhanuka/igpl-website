Location

Taloja — India's Chemical Hub

IGPL's manufacturing campus is located in Taloja MIDC, Raigad District, Maharashtra — approximately 50 km from JNPT (Jawaharlal Nehru Port Trust), India's largest container port. This proximity enables efficient import of o-xylene feedstock and export of finished products to global markets.
