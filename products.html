Core Product

Phthalic Anhydride (PA)

CAS 85-44-9  |  Molecular Formula: C8H4O3

What It Is

Phthalic Anhydride is an organic compound and one of the most widely used industrial chemicals in the world. It appears as snow-white, free-flowing flakes with a characteristic acrid odour. It is the principal commercial form of phthalic acid and an essential intermediate in the chemical value chain.

What It Is Used For

PA is the primary feedstock for plasticizers — particularly DOP (Dioctyl Phthalate), DINP, and DBP — which make PVC flexible for pipes, cables, flooring, and synthetic leather. It is also used in unsaturated polyester resins (construction, automotive), alkyd resins (paints and coatings), dyes and pigments, and pharmaceutical intermediates.

Why IGPL

IGPL operates 270,000 MTPA of PA capacity across five production lines at Taloja, Maharashtra — the largest single-site PA complex in India. We hold approximately 50% of India's PA market. Our plants run on the Wacker Von-Heyden Low Energy Process, engineered by Lurgi GmbH, Germany — the same technology used by leading global PA producers. We supply on annual contracts to India's major plasticizer, resin, and pigment manufacturers.

MALEIC ANHYDRIDE
CAS 108-31-6

What It Is

Maleic Anhydride is an organic compound produced by IGPL through recovery from PA process wash water. It is an important chemical intermediate with diverse applications across multiple industries.

What It Is Used For

Primary feedstock for unsaturated polyester resins (UPR). Also used in agrochemicals (pesticide formulations), food industry applications (fumaric acid, malic acid), lubricant additives, personal care products, and water treatment chemicals.

Why IGPL

Our MA is recovered from an integrated PA production process, ensuring consistent supply and competitive pricing. IGPL is a reliable domestic source for Indian MA consumers who would otherwise depend on imports.

BENZOIC ACID
CAS 65-85-0

What It Is

Benzoic Acid is an aromatic carboxylic acid recovered as a byproduct from IGPL's PA production process. Available in technical grade, it serves as a versatile chemical intermediate.

What It Is Used For

Used in perfumes and fragrances (as benzyl benzoate), dyes and pigments, plasticizers, preservatives (sodium benzoate for food and personal care), alkyd resins, and pharmaceutical synthesis.

Why IGPL

Our BA is produced as an integrated byproduct, not from a standalone process — meaning we can offer competitive pricing with reliable supply. Technical grade BA from IGPL meets the specifications required by dye, resin, and chemical manufacturers.

DIETHYL PHTHALATE (DEP)
CAS 84-66-2

What It Is

Diethyl Phthalate is a clear, colourless liquid plasticizer produced by IGPL as a downstream value-added product from our own Phthalic Anhydride. It represents our move into higher-margin finished chemical products.

What It Is Used For

Used as a plasticizer in PVC processing, as a solvent and fixative in perfumes and cosmetics, in pharmaceutical coatings and tablet manufacturing, in cellulose acetate formulations, and in fragrance retention for personal care products.

Why IGPL

IGPL manufactures DEP using our own PA as feedstock — giving us complete vertical integration from raw material to finished plasticizer. This ensures supply security, consistent quality, and competitive pricing for our DEP customers.
