Our Products

A Complete Chemical Portfolio

From India's largest PA complex — core products, recovered byproducts, and downstream specialties.

Portfolio

Four Products. One Integrated Value Chain.

IGPL's product portfolio is structured around a single, tightly coupled process: ortho-xylene in, phthalic anhydride out — with byproducts recovered and downstream chemistry added. Every product in our portfolio traces back to the same reactor system at Taloja.

Our Process

One Site. Four Products. Integrated.

IGPL's product portfolio originates from a single oxidation process at its Taloja campus. This integration means consistent quality, efficient byproduct recovery, and a supply chain that is compact and controllable. The same site that produces 270,000 MTPA of PA also recovers MA and BA, and adds DEP downstream.
