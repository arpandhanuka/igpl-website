Careers

Work at the Centre of India's Chemical Value Chain

IGPL operates India's largest PA manufacturing complex. Our teams manage real plants, complex processes, and a supply chain that reaches every corner of the Indian economy.

Why IGPL

Serious Work.
Real Ownership.

We operate five chemical plants continuously, 365 days a year. The engineers, operators, and analysts who join us are responsible for real systems — not simulations or support tickets. When a reactor parameter drifts, or a quality batch needs to be held, or a logistics window opens at JNPT, the decision matters.

IGPL has been in continuous operation since 1992. That longevity means our best people have built careers here — not just jobs. We have process engineers who have been with us for over two decades. We have commercial managers who know every plasticizer compounder in Gujarat and Rajasthan. We are not a startup.

We are also not a company in decline. IGPL is a listed market leader investing in operations, systems, and people who can execute at scale.

Career Areas

Where You Could Work

Process Engineering

Design, optimise, and troubleshoot catalytic oxidation processes across five PA plants. Heat and mass balance, catalyst management, yield optimization. Chemical engineers with process focus.

Plant Operations

24×7 DCS-managed continuous operations. Shift supervision, control room operation, startup/shutdown procedures, abnormal situation management. Diploma and B.Tech operators.

Quality & Laboratory

Chemical analysis, in-process testing, product QC, TDS/SDS compliance, and customer specification management. Analytical chemists and QC technicians.

Commercial & Sales

Domestic and export sales of PA, MA, BA, and DEP. Customer relationships across plasticizer, resin, and specialty chemical sectors. Logistics coordination with JNPT and road transport.

Finance & Accounts

Management accounting, costing, working capital management, and statutory compliance for a listed chemical manufacturer.

Corporate Functions

HR, legal, corporate affairs, IT, and investor relations. Supporting a ~₹2,400 Cr market-cap listed company.

Our Environment

Technical. Demanding.
Continuous.

Our Taloja campus operates on 3-shift continuous cycles. The DCS control rooms are the nerve center — real-time process data, alarm management, and operator decision-making happening simultaneously across all five plants.

The laboratory runs full shift coverage for in-process testing. The commercial team works across time zones on export logistics. The maintenance engineers plan shutdowns months in advance and execute them to tight windows.

Culture

What We Value

Operational Excellence

We measure what matters: yield, energy per tonne, on-spec rate, cycle time. Performance is data-driven.

Technical Depth

The best careers here belong to people who want to understand the process, not just manage it.

Reliability

A chemical plant does not stop. Neither do the teams that run it. Consistency under pressure is valued above everything.

Growth

IGPL is a growing platform. The downstream expansion and export opportunity creates real scope for individuals who want to grow with the business.
