Why IGPL

Work That Matters at Scale

IGPL runs five Phthalic Anhydride plants on a single site — the largest such complex in India. That means our engineers, operators, and analysts work on equipment, processes, and problems that few other facilities in the country can offer.

When you join IGPL, you join a team that produces 270,000 tonnes of specialty chemicals per year, using technology licensed from Wacker Chemie, Germany. You operate DCS-controlled reactors, troubleshoot catalyst performance, manage continuous process streams, and maintain infrastructure that India's plastics, coatings, and resin industries depend on.

What You Work With

  • Wacker Chemie-licensed PA production technology
  • Distributed Control Systems (DCS) for real-time process monitoring
  • ISO 9001:2015 quality management systems
  • Steam recovery and cogeneration infrastructure
  • In-house laboratory for quality testing and R&D

Who Thrives Here

Roles That Build Real Expertise

Chemical & Process Engineers

Run reaction optimisation, troubleshoot catalyst behaviour, improve yields. You'll own process parameters for continuous units, not just monitor screens.

Plant Operators

Manage DCS operations across five PA plants. Learn continuous process control at industrial scale — handling reactor startups, shutdown sequences, and abnormal situation management.

Quality & Lab Analysts

Test every batch. Calibrate instruments. Ensure that PA, MA, Benzoic Acid, and DEP shipments meet spec. Your work directly affects product acceptance by downstream manufacturers.

Operations & Maintenance Managers

Lead shift teams. Plan turnarounds. Coordinate between production, maintenance, and safety. Build the operational leadership that keeps a complex of this scale running reliably.

Long-Term Growth

A Career, Not Just a Job

IGPL is not a company where you'll rotate through departments for two years and leave. We are a manufacturing company with deep operational knowledge, and our people build expertise over years — in process chemistry, equipment reliability, safety management, and plant operations.

Many of our senior engineers and managers have been with IGPL for 15+ years. They know the plants intimately. That institutional knowledge is our competitive advantage, and we invest in growing it.

On-the-job training with experienced operators and engineers
Technical training on Wacker-licensed process technology
Safety certifications and regulatory compliance training
Exposure to plant expansion and capital project execution
Clear progression from trainee to shift-in-charge to plant head
