Market Position

India's Largest Single-Site PA Complex

IGPL operates five Phthalic Anhydride plants at a single location in Taloja, Maharashtra, with a combined installed capacity of 270,000 MTPA. We hold approximately 50% of India's PA market.

Our production technology is licensed from Wacker Chemie, Germany. Our Taloja facility is 50 km from JNPT, giving us direct access to port logistics for raw material imports and product exports.
